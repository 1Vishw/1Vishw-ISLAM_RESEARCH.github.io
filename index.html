Important People in Islam

Prophet Muhammad

Early Life:

Prophet Muhammad was born in Mecca, Saudi Arabia in 570 AD (570 years after Jesus, we are currently in 2023 AD). He was the Final Prophet of Islam. Prophet Muhammad was a very Honest and trustworthy.


Oringin of Islam in his Life:

In 610 AD Prophet Muhammad was meditating in Mount Hira, then Angle Gabriel visited him who told him about the Allah. Allah sent his words to world through angle Gabriel. Muhammad wrote the words of Allah, and it becomes Quran. Muslims believe that he continuous write it till the rest of his life.

Problems:

Muhammad life was threatened by the people in power in Mecca. Because of this treat he took his followers on a journey form Mecca to Medina in 622 AD. This journey was called Hijrah (Migration). It was so important for Islam because it was the year when Islamic Calendar begins. After in living in Medina for 10 years and making lot of followers he was able to go back to Mecca in 632 AD.

Adam

THE FIRST MAN

Adam was the first Prophet of Allah, and he was the first human created by Allah. Quran describes that Allah created Adam with the help of Clay and Dust. Adam and his wife Hawwa (Eve) were sent to a paradise called Jannah.

Why was he sent to Earth??

In Jannah it was forbidden to eat from a specific tree. But, due to the influence of Satan, they were forced to eat the fruits of tree. Because of his act they both were sent to Earth as a punishment. However, Allah forgave Adam and his wife Hawwa.

Abu Bakr

Why he is important??

He was one of the closest companions and advisor of Prophet Muhammad. He was the one of the earliest Muslim.

Holy Places

This is one of the Holiest Place of Muslim. The most interesting point about this Masjid is that there is a black stone (hajr-e-aswad) in this masjid. The story about this stone is that this stone was fallen from Jannah, it is the place where Adam and Hawwa used to live.




Al-Masjid an-Nabawi

This mosque used to be Muhammad House in Medina when he went to Medina due to treats of his life.

AL-MASJID AL-AQSA

Muslims believe that Prophet Muhammad was transported from Al-Haram to Al-Aqsa and then to heaven. It is also called as the second house of Allah on Earth.
